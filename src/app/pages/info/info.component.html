Welcome to "Tic Tac Toe"

You can start playing Tic Tac Toe by clicking on an empty field. In the upper left corner, you can see who's turn it is.
The first player to place three items in a row, column, or diagonal wins the game.
If Green wins, a green screen appears, as you can see on the left. Analog if red wins.
In case of a draw, a white screen will appear.
